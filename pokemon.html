Pokémon Project

"Catch them all ! Pokémon site"

Members : Kim Harribaud
Duration : Two months
Specifications : Without
Situation : Personal
Difficulty : Medium

Detail and operation :

I personally carried out a project during the shortage of Pokémon Boosters by creating an online commerce website dedicated to buying and selling sealed Pokémon boosters and boxes. The website had several sections and pages allowing users to navigate between new and old products according to their preferences.

To track sales and product uploads, I used an Excel spreadsheet to manage transactions and calculate revenue. This project was designed and developed in two months, and was active for one month before being closed. Despite its short lifespan, this project allowed me to develop my skills in web design, e-commerce management, and sales tracking.

Additionally, it gave me the opportunity to meet the growing demand for sealed Pokémon boosters and boxes during a shortage while providing a user-friendly and easy shopping experience for users.

This project allowed me to develop my knowledge in web development, as well as in stock management and online marketing.

Throughout the process of creating and managing the online commerce website, I gained valuable experience in web development techniques, such as creating user-friendly interfaces, optimizing site speed and performance, and ensuring secure transactions.

Additionally, I developed my skills in managing stock inventory, which involved tracking product availability, managing orders, and ensuring timely delivery to customers. Furthermore, by marketing the products and promoting the website online, I was able to enhance my knowledge and skills in digital marketing.

This included using various online marketing strategies, such as search engine optimization, social media marketing, and email marketing, to drive traffic to the website and increase sales. Overall, this project was a great opportunity for me to learn and improve my skills in various aspects of web development, stock management, and online marketing, which are all essential for success in the world of e-commerce.
